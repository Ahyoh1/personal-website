Flagship products

AI customer-intelligence platform

Pinpoint · Analytics Engineering Manager

Problem: customer activity was scattered across systems, and no one had a real-time view of what was happening with any given account.

Approach: an event-driven pipeline that triggers LLM summarisation in Vertex AI as events occur, scaled across thousands of events a day, served through a natural-language interface across a web app, a CRM-embedded app and an MCP server.

Outcome: adopted company-wide as the default way teams check in on a customer.

Pricing & sell-propensity intelligence

Zoopla · Analytics Engineer

Problem: Customer Success advice on pricing and sell propensity was gut-feel, not data-driven.

Approach: joined historical sales data with public housing data via dbt, served through a real-time web UI for branch talking points and a Tableau dashboard for listing-level recommendations.

Outcome: contributed to a strong revenue uplift across upsell and new revenue within six months of launch.
