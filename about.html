About

Data was never the plan. It's just where the questions led.

I started in human physiology, then neuroscience, and then…

In 2020, during the COVID-19 pandemic, I stumbled into data. I loved it so much that I spent around 18 hours a day, for 4 months, learning the ropes of data analysis, data visualisation and machine learning.

That took me from a small room in Nigeria to the UK's fastest-growing startup between 2019 and 2022 (Blue Motor Finance), Chelsea Football Club, Commercetools, Zoopla, and now to Pinpoint, where I lead analytics engineering, defining what a data platform looks like, and shipping AI-powered data products that drive business revenue.

Aside from burying my head in data platforms, I have trained over 5,000 data analysts, and I spend my free time travelling, exploring and playing the guitar.
